What's a Flub Club?

A monthly event where hiphop heads meet to practice on stage and get feedback on their performance, have fun and socialize.

How's it Work?

  1. Sign up on arrival
  2. Watch others perform, give feedback
  3. Your turn: name the song & describe the feedback you want
  4. Perform your song
  5. Get helpful feedback on your performance
  6. Build connections, grow NoCo hip-hop.

F.A.Q.

Anyone 18+ can attend. IDs are checked at the door.

There is not cover charge, but you are encouraged to donate a few bucks each session to cover the costs of promotion and future growth. Please donate while RSVPing on Eventbrite.

Each person can perform one song per club meeting. If everyone present has performed and there is more time, the moderator may allow more performances.

Any hiphop, rap, trap or related music is welcome, like R&B, reggae, trip-hop, soul, motown, lo-fi, chill-hop, etc.

So that everyone gets their chance, we encourage you to choose a song that is under 6 minutes.

Alcohol is served to 21+ at The Coast. Non-alcoholic beverages are available for purchase. No beverages are supplied free of charge. (Please support our generous hosts.)

Profanity is allowed.

No hate speech of any kind will be tolerated. Moderators take action on a case-by-case basis.

You were whack once, too. Give the feedback you needed back then. We’re here to build up, not tear down.

It's best to email it ahead of time to noco@flub.club but we can still play it if you have it on your phone. (We have the right connection cable.)
